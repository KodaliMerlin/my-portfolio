Selected Projects

Scholar Management Mobile App

An end-to-end mobile app designed to replace an inefficient Google Forms system.

The Problem

The existing system was manual, clunky, and frustrating. It relied on disconnected Google Forms, causing confusion for students and hours of manual data entry for administrators.

My Role & Process

As lead UI/UX Designer and developer, I followed a full user-centered design process:

  1. Research & Wireframing: Conducted user interviews and mapped core user flows in Figma.
  2. Prototyping & Testing: Built a high-fidelity, interactive prototype and conducted usability testing with 5 scholars to validate the design.
  3. Development: Developed the final cross-platform mobile app using Dart (Flutter) and Firebase.

Financial Needs Application (SIH 2024)

A full-stack mobile application to help rural demographics find optimal financial schemes.

The Problem

Finding the right government financial schemes is an overwhelming process for rural demographics with varying levels of digital literacy.

My Role & Process

As the lead UI/UX Designer for the Smart India Hackathon team, my goal was to design an extremely simple and trustworthy interface.

  • Design: Created user personas and high-fidelity, accessible prototypes in Figma.
  • Tech: The app used a React Native interface, a Flask/ML backend, and Looker Studio for data visualization, which I also designed.
